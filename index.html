Letters Across the Sky

For the one whose voice crosses oceans…

A whispered vow beneath the endless sky, my heart’s devotion written in every star above…

In the quiet moments, when the world fades away, I find myself thinking of you...

Your voice, a melody that dances through my mind, echoes like a gentle breeze across the ocean...

Each word you speak, a note in the symphony of my heart, resonates with a warmth that transcends distance...

In the stillness of the night, I close my eyes and imagine your laughter, a sound that brings light to my darkest days...

Your presence, though miles away, feels like a soft embrace, wrapping me in a cocoon of love and comfort...

Every time we meet, it’s as if the universe conspires to bring us closer, erasing the distance between us...

Six meetings, our little Hexad, yet enough for my heart to know... Across time zones and oceans, I’m still right beside you...

May these words carry my love across the miles...

With all my love,

Your Serendipity

Us one day!
